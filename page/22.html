How to be kind to oneself

13th April 2018

"You can't feel dumb for not knowing how to launch a rocket."

I got some helpful perspective from a friend today about how harshly I criticise myself for not being able to solve problems that aren't problems I've ever learnt to solve.

Perhaps I have been an ambitious person throughout my life and so far it has worked well, but sometimes I can be a little bit too ambitious.

I guess it is important to acknowledge my own strengths and abilities, and to not berate myself for not knowing how to solve everything under thr sun.

This could be how to be kinder to myself. I am unsure, I am still figuring out what the line is between accepting my lack of knowledge, and pushing myself to know more.

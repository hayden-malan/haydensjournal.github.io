A new dawn

28th March 2018

The other day I went to the sea:

I was going to get some silence and some peace. Nature is good like that.

I was sitting at the shore doing 5 minutes of mindfulness and a thought came to mind: "wow, there is so much maths happening all around me".

I felt really small all of a sudden and humbled by the things I felt I could programme vs nature's ridiculous amount of detail that was unfolding 24/7 right where I was sitting.

Moving is challenging.

12th April 2018

Especially this time.

I finally moved my bed today. It's been in the same place for the last 2 years.

But this is more than a move from 2 years of living one place.

This is the move. I am moving out, and venturing into the world of renting. For the first time in my whole life. Exciting stuff. Challenging stuff.

It only really sunk in once my bed had been moved today. It hit harder than I thought it would.

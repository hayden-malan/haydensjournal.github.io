In control

More and more people have been telling me recently, that I am in control. In control of my emotions. Of my situation. Of my life. Very empowering news.

I have been striving to understand it more, as for me it is a huge stress from my previous understandings. In the English language, when someone is sad, usually I would ask "Why are you sad?" or "What made you sad?". I would certainly not ask "Why are you choosing to be sad?".

But now I have been encouraged to ask myself "Why are you choosing to be sad?". I've been advised to take control. To choose to be happy.

I am currently trying my best to implement this as much as I can. That is all for the day.

Getting out is important.

6th April 2018

A change in perspective helps.

Often I can get trapped in a sense of disconnectedness.

It's not something that gets in the way of things I want to do. Provided I have things I want to do.

It was really great going outside today to talk to my friends. Even the small things like that can help. No big fuss had to be made, all I needed was a reason to get some fresh air.

I am grateful for my friends. They make my world a happier place.
